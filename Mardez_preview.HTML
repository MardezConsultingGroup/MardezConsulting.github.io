Services

Five lines of work, one discipline.

  1. SVC-01 29 CFR 1910 / 1926

    OSHA Compliance

    Program development, gap analysis, and audit prep for general industry and construction. Written procedures, inspection checklists, and corrective-action tracking your people can actually use.

  2. SVC-02 NIST / physical + cyber

    Risk & Security Assessment

    Threat and vulnerability assessments across physical and information security. Findings prioritized by likelihood and impact, with a remediation roadmap that fits the budget.

  3. SVC-03 S1000D / ASD-STE100

    Technical Writing

    Standards-compliant documentation: maintenance manuals, SOPs, and IETMs. Structured authoring, controlled language, and a publish pipeline built for revision control.

  4. SVC-04 infrastructure / security

    IT Consulting

    Practical infrastructure and security guidance for small teams and tribal agencies. Right-sized systems, hardening, and documentation that survives staff turnover.

  5. SVC-05 federal / municipal / tribal

    Government Contracting

    Capability positioning and compliance support for agencies and prime contractors. Built by people who write to the standard, not around it.
